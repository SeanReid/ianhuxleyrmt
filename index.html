Services

Do you need relief from low back pain, and/or headaches? Are you feeling the affects of poor posture? Your massage therapy appointment is only one phone call or email away. I can provide you with a thorough assessment to include; recommendation, treatment and prevention of all these conditions and more.

In 2014 I graduated as a Registered Massage Therapist (RMT), with over 2200 hours of training. After 20 years working in the health and social care field, this new career was a natural fit. My passion has always been people and continuous dedication to improving the general well being of my clients. ​

Each treatment is focused on the specific client need or area of concern. I provide a number of treatment modalities including Swedish techniques, deep tissue, myofascial release and trigger point therapy. ​

I practice out of two locations the city or, if you’re in the Leslieville/ Riverdale area, I can provide mobile services to your home. Should you require specific modifications I am happy to work with you to ensure your comfort during treatment.​

I am very much looking forward to being your RMT. If you have any questions about my services, please email or leave a message at 647.238.2036.​

I am a registered member of the College of Massage Therapists of Ontario (CMTO) and a member of the Registered Massage Therapist Association of Ontario (RMTAO)​

To make an appointment, please email or call me at: 647.238.2036​

Benefits of Massage Therapy can include:​

  • Reduced muscle tension​
  • Stress reduction​
  • Improved range of motion​
  • Increased circulation​
  • Pain reduction​
  • Increased joint mobility​

Conditions for which Massage can be beneficial include:

  • Strains and sprains​
  • Anxiety and Depression​
  • Sports injuries​
  • Plantar Faciitis​
  • Carpal Tunnel Syndrome​
  • Headaches​
  • General muscle pain​
  • Repetitive Strain injuries
