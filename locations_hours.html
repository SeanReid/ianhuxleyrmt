Cancellations​

I ask that, where possible, you provide me with 24 hours notice of a cancellation. This allows me to be able to offer the appointment to another client.​

In the event you are unable to keep your appointment and I am provided with less than 24 hours notice, you will be required to pay for the full cost of treatment, I realise that some things are out of our control, such as an illness, and in that event an exception may be made.​

Insurance

Many extended health plans cover massage therapy treatments provided by a registered massage therapist. Most no longer require a doctor’s note for treatment. However, policies can differ. Please check your plan for further information.

Receipts

Receipts issued for reimbursement can only be given in the name of the person receiving the treatment. If you are purchasing a gift certificate or paying on behalf of someone else, this will be stated on the receipt.​

Privacy and Confidentiality​

As a Registered Massage Therapist, I follow strict privacy legislation. Any personal information that you share with me before, during, or after your treatment will remain confidential. No information will be shared with an outside party unless your written permission is given or the information is required by law.
